About Us

Matabungkay Beach Hotel is located on the Beach Front of Brgy. Matabungkay, Lian, Batangas. The hotel was established during the 1980s and still the top of mind venue for wedding, debut, family gathering and corporate events. Matabungkay Beach Hotel is famous for the trees and bushes that surround the beach house and villas in the resort, crystal clear shore and the white sand along its coastline. Matabungkay Beach Hotel also serves a great venue to watch and capture the mesmerizing Batangas sunset and sunrise.
